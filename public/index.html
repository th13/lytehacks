What is LyteHacks

LyteHacks was born out of a need to further develop the hackathon culture here in Tallahassee. For every academic semester there is a “season” that allows the hackers to gain points through attending and creating awesome hacks. Each season consists of 3 hackathon events and an expo to close out the season.

During each event the hackers form teams and have 10 hours to create anything they would like from scratch. The expo is the time for the hackers, sponsors, and other local businesses to interact and network with one another and see the innovative projects that came out of the season.

What is LyteHacks

For every academic semester there is a “season” that allows the hackers to gain points through attending and creating awesome hacks. Each season consists of 3 hackathon events and an expo to close out the season.

What Is a Hackathon

An event that brings in software and hardware engineers, designers, and other inspired students to create team projects, or hacks, from scratch. The projects created through this type of collaboration are truly inspiring and some go on to be very successful.

Student hackathons have exploded throughout the US and a Hacker Culture has formed. The sense of community is unrivaled from any other kind of event in the nation and it has been formed from the common goal to hack. To be a hacker comes with an attitude of building to learn, working together, and the desire to create. To see how inspiring this community really is look at some blog posts, written by the community.
